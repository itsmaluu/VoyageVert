Our Tips

Flight Booking Tips:

  • Stay flexible with travel dates and times.
  • Consider nearby airports for better deals.
  • Set up fare alerts to monitor price changes.
  • Explore last-minute deals for potential discounts.
  • Check for student discounts offered by airlines.
  • Consider alternative transportation modes such as trains, buses, or ridesharing services.
  • Opt for eco-friendly and budget-friendly transportation options.
  • Choose flexible booking options and off-peak travel times for savings.
  • Plan and stay flexible to enjoy affordable and sustainable travel experiences.

Note: Google Flights is a great tool for booking flights as it offers various features and options to find the best deals.

Hotel Booking

  • If you're looking to save on accommodations, consider opting for hostels or Airbnbs over hotels.
  • Hostels often offer budget-friendly dormitory options with communal spaces, perfect for meeting fellow travelers.
  • Meanwhile, Airbnbs provide unique and affordable stays in local neighborhoods, offering a more authentic experience.
  • By choosing hostels or Airbnbs, you not only save money but also have the opportunity to immerse yourself in the local culture and connect with locals and other travelers.
  • So next time you're booking a trip, give hostels or Airbnbs a try for a more budget-friendly and enriching travel experience.

Note: Airbnb is a great tool for booking hostels/houses as it offers various features and options to find the best deals.
